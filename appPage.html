Welcome to BOIngredients



It is the end of the semester, and you have this random piece of meat you found in your freezer when you were cleaning it out. And you realize that it is about to expire. But your like, "Oh no! I don't know what to make with this!". That is when BOIngredients comes in handy! Just enter what meat it is and click on "Make Me Food". It will then show you all of the delicious foods you can make using that piece of meat. You can even click on the picture or the title and it will take you to Big Oven where you can find out how to make that heavenly meal. So explore and enjoy and make meals that will make you the talk of the evening.
